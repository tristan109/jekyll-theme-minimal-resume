Here is a track I submitted to a production contest hosted by a discord community I'm a part of. The theme of the contest was "record your own samples." Here is the description I submitted with the track: This track is a testament to the versatility of the stock Ableton sampler and the iZotope Alloy drum presets. The goal was to make a no-sacrifice track with modulation and compression picking up the slack where my sampling skills failed. Samples included: lots of layered clapping, tapping a coffee mug, dropping my water bottle on my desk, and stomping on my floor.
